# Code of Conduct

Boon is an open-source project for gratitude tipping and agent-assisted funding.
Keep collaboration clear, respectful, and focused on making the system safer.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful of different backgrounds and experience levels.
- Assume good intent, but be precise when discussing risk, money movement, or security.
- Keep criticism tied to code, docs, tests, or product behavior.
- Disclose conflicts of interest when they affect protocol, wallet, or vendor choices.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private keys, OAuth secrets, personal data, or exploit details.
- Pressuring maintainers to rush security-sensitive changes without review.
- Spam, phishing, or impersonation attempts.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may hide, edit, or remove comments and may temporarily or permanently
block contributors for behavior that makes the project unsafe or unproductive.
For sensitive conduct issues, use the same private reporting path described in
[SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) and mark the report as conduct-related.
